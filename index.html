Cycling Tour

This tour will be the best option for you if you want to see local lives and beautiful landscapes through the Siem Reap countryside. We make lots of stops to see the daily villages and farms and to enjoy some delicious local snacks. Let's explore the local villages with Onederz. You will know how beautiful Cambodia is.

Street Food Tour

Experience Cambodian Streed Food Culture. Taste the best of Siem Reap's street food on a 3-hour evening tour with our staff! On this epic food adventure, we will hop on local transport and travel away from the city center to sample authentic Siem Reap snacks, desserts, and Khmer cuisine staples. Delicious, authentic, and something you don't want to miss.

Yoga Class

Yoga brings the body and mind together and is built on three main elements – movement, breathing and meditation. Yoga has many physical and mental health benefits including improved posture, flexibility, strength, balance and body awareness.
