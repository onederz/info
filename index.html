Koh Ker : Koh Ker is located 130km from Siem Reap, and visiting there is easily combined with a trip to Beng Mealea. The main temple at Koh Ker is a 7-tier step-pyramid, and the views from the top are a very rewarding experience.

Phare Circus : Siem Reap's most unique, authentic, and top-rated evening entertainment. More than just a circus, Phare performers use theater, music, dance, and modern circus arts to tell uniquely Cambodian stories, historical, folk, and modern. The young circus artists will astonish you with their energy, emotion, enthusiasm, and talent.
